`/ccfold` merges upstream CLAUDE.md template changes into your project's local copy, preserving project-specific content like Dev-Team and custom sections. Use it when the ccwork kit gets updated and you want to pull new rules or sections without losing your customizations. It also generates `.claude-project.md` with cached platform detection. For more on how CLAUDE.md works, see `/ccwork tour` or `docs/concepts.md`.
